6th - 8th DECEMBER, 2019

Fane is proud to announce the line-up for the inaugural Words Weekend. There will be over 60 events taking place across 5 venues of the iconic Sage Gateshead from 6-8 December 2019. Every event is accessible, BSL interpreted and 40% of events are completely free.
