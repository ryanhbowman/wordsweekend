Where words come to life

A new words-themed festival taking place at a series of unique settings across Britain. WORDS WEEKEND will celebrate all things words through a variety of exciting curated live shows over a multi-day event. Our first festival will take place at SAGE GATESHEAD from 25–27 th October 2019, followed by THE LOWRY, SALFORD in March 2020 and REGENT’S PARK OPEN AIR THEATRE in summer 2020.

Each festival will showcase local talent and business, as well as bring to audiences a diverse and eclectic mix of speakers and shows. DIVERSITY and ACCESSIBILITY are at the heart of Words Weekend; as well as our programming, we are striving to make all events handicap accessible and BSL interpreted.
