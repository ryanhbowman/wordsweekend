A dynamic new festival celebrating the power of words, Words Weekend will host a variety of innovative live shows in a series of unique settings across the UK.

Each festival will showcase local and international talent and bring to audiences an inspiring mix of speakers and shows over multiple days. Diversity and accessibility are at the heart of our programming with over 25% of events being free, and all events handicap accessible and BSL interpreted. Helping us to deliver this vision are our Words Weekend ambassadors: David Olusoga, Liv Little and Karolina Sutton.
